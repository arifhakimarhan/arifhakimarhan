Frequently Asked Questions

Will my items be dry cleaned or laundered?
Items will be dry cleaned or laundered based on fabric care labels.
Can I send items without a care label?
Yes, we will determine the best care process for these items.
Will my items be hung or folded?
You can choose to have your items hung or folded as per your preference.
What if my shirt has a detachable collar or stiffened front?
Special care will be taken for items with detachable parts or stiffened fronts.
